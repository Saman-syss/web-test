Who are we?

Devdaha EBHS is one of the most reputed institution in the Rupandehi District. Our mission is to support our students to realize their own potential and accomplish their objectives while keeping the spirit of togetherness.

Mission

To strive for quality education in keeping with the motto of the college, "open up your future" and empower learners with transdisciplinary knowledge & skills to compete in the 21st century.

Vision

To create leaders of the future, equipped with the skills of tomorrow, entrepreneurs, and responsible citizens.

Couursess we offer

At DEBHS, we believe in fostering a nurturing educational environment that empowers students to reach their full potential. Our comprehensive range of courses spans across primary, basic, and secondary education, ensuring a well-rounded and enriching learning experience for every student.

Primary

In our Primary Education program, we focus on laying a strong foundation for academic success and personal growth. Our dedicated faculty employs innovative teaching methods to make learning enjoyable, fostering curiosity and critical thinking. Subjects such as Mathematics, English, Science, and Social Studies are taught in an interactive and engaging manner, preparing students for the exciting journey ahead.

BASIC

As students progress into Basic Education, our curriculum expands to include a diverse range of subjects that not only strengthen academic prowess but also build essential life skills. In addition to core subjects, students explore areas like Arts, Physical Education, and Languages, encouraging a holistic approach to education. Our committed educators guide students through this crucial phase, emphasizing both knowledge acquisition and character development.

Secondary

In Secondary Education, our focus shifts towards preparing students for the challenges of higher education and beyond. We offer a wide array of subjects, allowing students to tailor their education to their interests and career aspirations . At DEBHS we are committed to providing a conducive learning environment that goes beyond traditional classrooms. Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to enhance the educational experience, promote holistic development, and foster a sense of community. EXCLUSIVE LIBRARYS

Step into our well-equipped library, and you'll discover a treasure trove of knowledge. Our extensive collection spans a wide range of genres and subjects, catering to diverse interests and academic pursuits. The library serves as a quiet haven for students to delve into the world of literature, conduct research, and explore their intellectual curiosity. Our dedicated librarians are alway s on hand to guide students in their quest for knowledge and provide a nurturing space for both individual study and collaborative learning.

TECHNICAL LAB

In an era driven by technology, our cutting-edge computer lab is at the forefront of preparing students for the digital landscape. Here, students have access to the latest technology and software, empowering them to develop crucial digital literacy skills. Our computer lab is not only a space for learning coding, programming, and digital applications but also a hub for innovation and creativity. Our experienced instructors guide students through the world of technology, ensuring they are well-prepared for the challenges and opportunities of the digital age.
